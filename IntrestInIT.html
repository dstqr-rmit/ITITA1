Interest in IT

What is your interest in IT?

My major interests in Information Technology are coding, developing ideas & concepts into reality, and solving IT issues that require large IT expertise. I have loved expanding my knowledge in the IT field for quite some time and know that there is so much more out there that I can learn and share for the benefit of others. 

 

When did your interest in IT start?

My vast interest in IT began in Year 9 Information Technology classes. Although from a young age I would always be interested in learning new things about IT and code, taking IT classes really allowed me to expand on my knowledge in the field and grow my skills further over the years.

 

Was there a particular event or person that sparked your interest? 

To summarise, no there was no particular event or person who sparked my interest in the IT field. More so, I believe it was a personal interest that allowed me to continually develop and grow my interest over the past years.

 

Outline your IT experience (if any).

I do not have any IT-specific experience other than running the media team at my church on Sunday’s. In summary, very minimal applied IT experience.

 

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

I chose to enrol in RMIT as I have heard RMIT will provide me with an education that prepares me to succeed, exposes me to new ideas and equips me with the skills and knowledge to excel in my IT career. 

 

What do you expect to learn during your studies?

Through my studies, I expect to learn valuable skills which I can utilise and apply in IT-related carers. I also expect myself to be able to develop full-functioning apps (with my own programming) which will have a positive influence on many people’s lives.
